Minimum Requirements (To run LuminOS + Lumin)

  • Processor: 6 Cores or higher (Must support AVX2 instructions)
  • RAM: 8 GB+ (The AI model requires ~5GB of free RAM to load).
  • Storage: 64GB+ free space (use of an SSD highly recommended).
  • GPU/Graphics: NVIDIA/AMD Dedicated GPU (Accelerates Lumin's response time); integrated graphics will also work.
